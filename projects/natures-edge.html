Project Overview

Craftsmanship deserves a worthy canvas

Nature's Edge Carpentry is a bespoke woodworking studio dedicated to crafting pieces that celebrate the inherent beauty of natural wood — from custom furniture and home decor to outdoor creations. The studio blends nature-inspired design with decades of artisan expertise, with an unwavering commitment to sustainability and precision.

The design challenge was to translate that physical craftsmanship into a digital experience that felt equally considered. The web presence needed to communicate premium quality and trust while remaining warm and approachable — not corporate, not rustic, but genuinely artisanal.

The project covered four pages — a cinematic homepage, an About page that built brand story and team credibility, a Portfolio page organised by category, and a Contact page for client enquiries.

The Challenge

Translating craft into pixels

Artisan businesses face a specific design tension: their work is deeply physical — the weight of wood, the texture of grain, the smell of sawdust — and a website can only approximate that. Nature's Edge Carpentry needed a design that evoked all of that sensory richness through colour, photography, and layout alone.

The brief called for a website that would attract high-value clients — homeowners and interior designers seeking bespoke pieces — while remaining approachable enough for first-time enquiries. Premium but never cold. Skilled but never intimidating.

Four pages, each with a distinct purpose, had to feel unified by a single visual language rooted in the materials the studio works with every day.

Home

Cinematic full-bleed hero with a craftsman at work. Four brand pillars in a terracotta strip. A full-width woodworking quote section. Customer reviews carousel with botanical background.

About

Our Story, Our Mission, Our Vision — three sections building brand depth through photography and text. Closes with Meet the Team to put real faces behind the craft.

Portfolio

Work organised into three categories: Furnitures, Home Decor, and Outdoor Creations. Each category uses a 2-column image grid with a "See Projects" CTA to drive deeper engagement.

Contact

Studio address, phone, and email in a bold dark header. A clean enquiry form below with fields for name, email, subject, and message — simple and frictionless.

Design System

Colour, type & texture

Colour Palette

#3D1A0E Deep Brown
#A0522D Terracotta
#F5EDE6 Warm Cream
#FFFFFF White

Deep Brown (#3D1A0E) — The anchor colour, pulled directly from the darkest tones of aged hardwood. Used for the nav, footer, and contact header, it communicates depth, permanence, and premium quality without being harsh.

Terracotta (#A0522D) — The brand accent, evoking fired clay, autumn leaves, and the warm tones of freshly cut timber. Used for the brand pillars strip, hover states, and all key typographic highlights. Warm and confident.

Warm Cream (#F5EDE6) — The page background across all content sections. Avoids the coldness of pure white while keeping text legible. Feels like natural linen or unfinished wood — materials the studio works with daily.

Serif headings were chosen to echo the timeless, craft-oriented nature of the business. The weight and elegance of a good serif mirrors the quality the studio brings to every piece of wood.

Design Decisions

Key choices & rationale

Homepage Hero

The Craftsman, Not the Product

The hero photograph shows a craftsman at work, hands on wood, rather than a finished product. This was a deliberate choice — it communicates process and skill before outcome, immediately establishing that Nature's Edge is about craftsmanship, not just furniture.

Terracotta Strip

Brand Values as Visual Anchor

The four brand pillars — Nature Inspired, Craftsmanship, Sustainability, Precision — are presented in a full-width terracotta section between the hero and the quote. This placement ensures every visitor sees the brand's core values before scrolling to products, framing all subsequent content through that lens.

Quote Section

Wood as the Medium

A full-bleed photograph of woodworking tools on a workbench carries an italic quote: "Woodworking, it is our specialty and our primary focus." The overlay serves as a tonal break between product content and reviews, and it anchors the brand's identity in the physical act of making.

Portfolio Structure

Categories Over Chronology

Work is organised into Furnitures, Home Decor, and Outdoor Creations — by project type rather than by date. This lets visitors self-select the category most relevant to their need, reducing time-to-relevance and improving the likelihood of an enquiry.

Reviews Section

Social Proof with Botanical Warmth

Customer reviews are displayed in a carousel over a dark botanical background — lush green foliage that brings nature directly into the testimonial experience. The "Reviews" label in a bold black block anchors the section without interrupting the visual flow.

About Page

Story, Mission, Vision, Team

The About page builds trust in a deliberate sequence: company history first, then mission, then vision, then the team. By the time the visitor reaches the team photos — Adetoun Daniel, Micheal Alabi, Sandra Tolu — they have already been given three reasons to believe in the studio's credibility.
