Project Overview

Health meets modern glamour

Better Oodles is a health and wellness brand on a mission to redefine how millennials approach well-being. Founded in 2017, the brand blends cutting-edge technology with time-honoured wellness principles to offer a curated range of products — from fitness wearables and nutritional supplements to meditation tools and yoga accessories.

The design challenge was to create a web experience that felt as aspirational as a lifestyle brand while remaining practical enough to drive product discovery and conversion. Health needed to feel glamorous, not clinical. Shopping needed to feel inspiring, not transactional.

The project covered three core pages — a bold, image-driven homepage, a structured shop page for product browsing, and a detailed about page that built brand trust through story and team.

The Challenge

Making wellness feel
desirable

Most health and wellness websites fall into one of two traps: they look too clinical and cold (trustworthy but unappealing), or too trendy and soft (appealing but unsubstantial). Better Oodles needed to escape both.

The brand targets millennials — a generation that expects digital experiences to feel premium, fast, and personal. The design needed to carry the energy of a fashion brand while communicating the credibility of a health company. Every page had to earn both trust and desire.

Innovation & Technology

Better Oodles integrates cutting-edge technology into its products. The UI needed to reflect this through bold imagery, confident typography, and a dark hero that signals premium quality.

Accessibility & Practicality

The brand's core commitment is making health accessible for millennials' daily lives. The shop page structure — with clear product cards, categories, and pricing — kept browsing effortless.

Community & Support

Beyond products, Better Oodles fosters a supportive wellness community. The about page and testimonial sections translated this into design through warmth, team portraits, and brand story.

Design System

Colour, type & energy

Colour Palette

#E8242A Brand Red
#0F0A0A Deep Black
#FDF0EE Blush
#FFFFFF White

Brand Red (#E8242A) — The hero colour. Red communicates energy, urgency, and passion — all aligned with fitness and health motivation. Used on the logo, CTAs, active nav states, and all key accent moments across the site.

Deep Black (#0F0A0A) — Used for the shop page hero to give a premium, editorial feel. Black anchors the brand as aspirational rather than cheap or mass-market.

Blush (#FDF0EE) — The homepage and about page background. Softens the brand for lifestyle and community content, making the red pop without aggression.

Design Decisions

Key choices & rationale

Homepage Hero

Two Backgrounds, One Brand

The homepage uses a soft blush background to feel warm and welcoming — lifestyle over e-commerce. The shop page flips to a full-bleed dark hero featuring the flagship QuantumFlex Fitness Bands, signalling premium product quality the moment you enter the store.

Product Cards

Scannable by Design

Product cards were kept minimal and consistent — image, name, sub-heading, and a single "Buy now" CTA. The grid system allows fast scanning without cognitive overload. Top Products and full grid sections were separated to create a browsing hierarchy.

Value Pillars

Show, Don't Just Tell

The homepage's three brand pillars — Innovation, Accessibility, Community — were each given circular product imagery overlaid at the top of the card. This grounded the abstract values in tangible product reality, making them feel earned rather than marketing copy.

About Page

Story Before Team

The About page leads with Company History, Our Mission, Innovative Approach, and Community Engagement before introducing the team. This structure builds brand credibility from the ground up — by the time you reach the team photos, you already believe in the company.

Quote Section

Motivational Interruption

The Aristotle quote — "We are what we repeatedly do. Excellence then is not an act but a habit." — appears as a full-bleed dark section between product content and the footer. It acts as an emotional reset, aligning the brand with a philosophy rather than just products.

Navigation

Clean & Persistent

The nav is minimal: logo, three links (Home, About, Shop), cart icon, and a blue "Contact Us" CTA button. The active page is underlined in red. Keeping navigation lightweight keeps the full-width product imagery and hero content as the visual priority on every page.
