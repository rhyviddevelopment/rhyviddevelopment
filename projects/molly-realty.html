Project Overview

One identity.
Two businesses.

Molly Realty and Leisure Ltd is a Nigerian company operating across two distinct verticals — real estate and agro-tourism. The client, Omolara Oyekunle, needed a visual identity that could credibly represent both sides of the business without splitting into two separate brands.

For the real estate audience, the brand needed to communicate security, trust, and professionalism. For the leisure and agro-tourism side, it needed to feel welcoming, natural, and adventure-ready. Finding a single mark that could hold both personalities was the central design challenge.

The Challenge

Bridging two worlds
in a single mark

Most dual-industry brands solve the problem by creating separate sub-brands or logos. The brief here required a different approach — a unified identity that doesn't compromise either side of the business.

The visual identity had to work for a property investor looking for a trustworthy agent, and equally for a family planning a nature retreat. Same logo. Same colours. Completely different contexts.

  • Professional and trustworthy for real estate clients
  • Welcoming and natural for leisure and agro-tourism
  • Memorable enough to stand alone as a monogram or icon
  • Versatile across print, digital, and signage applications

Target Audience

Designed for two very different people

🏠

Real Estate Audience

Individuals and families seeking to buy, sell, or rent property. They are evaluating trust and credibility — they need to feel confident that Molly Realty is a professional, established firm they can rely on for a major financial decision.

Needs: Trust · Authority · Stability
🌿

Leisure & Tourism Audience

Families, tourists, and adventure seekers interested in farm stays and nature-based activities. They are looking for an escape — they need the brand to feel warm, natural, and inviting, not corporate or transactional.

Needs: Warmth · Nature · Adventure

Process & Iteration

From sketch
to symbol

The initial phase focused on finding a visual solution that could unify two conceptually different ideas: Realty (security, structure, home) and Leisure (journey, path, nature).

Early sketches explored abstract combinations of a house silhouette and a winding road. The focus was on using negative space and line work to incorporate the brand initials ‘M’ and ‘R’ — which helped evolve the house structure into a unique, memorable mark.

The breakthrough came from the road element: a winding path that begins at the forefront of the house and curves inward — representing both a journey to a destination and the R of Realty. Two meanings, one line.

Design Rationale

Dual Interpretation

The logo mark functions as a single cohesive symbol. Depending on the viewer's context, it reads as either a house or a landmark with a path — making both audiences feel directly spoken to.

Realty Element

The overall shape is a sturdy, simplified house and shield — communicating security, protection, and trust. These are the essential values for real estate investment and property transactions.

Leisure Element

The path and road element curves inward from the forefront, representing a journey to a destination. This directly links the logo to the agro-tourism and leisure offerings of the business.

Design System

Colour, type & meaning

Colour Palette

#15381C Forest Green
#CEA52B Mustard Gold
Silver Road / Path

Deep Forest Green (#15381C) — The primary colour grounds the brand in trust, natural growth, and stability. It bridges the gap between land and property, making it equally at home on a property listing and an agro-tourism leaflet.

Mustard Gold (#CEA52B) — The secondary tone adds warmth, luxury, and high-quality leisure. It suggests premium experience and financial success — critical signals for a real estate brand, while also evoking harvests and sunshine for the leisure side.

Typography

Brand Name — Trajan Pro Bold
MOLLY

Trajan Pro Bold delivers a classic, established, and authoritative feel — instilling confidence, a key trait for a real estate brand. Its origins in classical Roman letterforms add gravitas without being cold.

Descriptor — Quicksand Regular
REALTY AND LEISURE LTD

Quicksand provides a modern geometric contrast to Trajan. Its clean, readable lines keep the full name legible at small sizes while offering a fresh, inviting tone that connects to the leisure and nature aspect of the brand.
